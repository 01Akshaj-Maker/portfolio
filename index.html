Kedovo Content Generator

the engine behind kedovo. an admin tool that takes raw NCERT textbook PDFs and generates 8 types of AI-powered educational content per chapter — fully automated, quality-tested, and published.

Admin ToolMulti-Model AIPipelineQA Framework

Admin Portal Modules

  • Metadata Management — boards, classes, subjects, chapters
  • Content Management — view, edit, publish generated content
  • Prompt Management — dynamic prompt templates per content type
  • Quality Testing Framework — 6-dimension validation
  • Curriculum Guidelines — board-specific rules & constraints
  • Assessment Generator — practice questions across 3 difficulty levels
  • Content Generator — end-to-end pipeline from PDF to published content

How It Works

  • Stage 1: PDF textbooks converted via Mathpix OCR to structured markdown
  • Stage 2: Multi-model AI generates 8 content types per chapter
  • Stage 3: Automated quality testing across 6 dimensions with auto-fix
  • Stage 4: Human review points, then auto-publish to Supabase

AI Orchestration

  • Multi-model support: Claude, Gemini, OpenAI
  • Smart model selection based on content type
  • Cost optimization with real-time token monitoring
  • Retry & recovery for consistent delivery
  • Board-agnostic architecture (CBSE, ICSE, State boards)
03

VaaniBooks — AI Audiobook Engine

AI platform that converts books to audiobooks in 11 indian languages with 25+ natural voices. built for the ministry of information & broadcasting. fully automated 5-stage pipeline.

Sarvam AIGeminiSupabaseMathpix OCR

5-Stage Pipeline

  • 01 Ingest — PDFs uploaded into the VaaniBooks pipeline
  • 02 Extract — Mathpix OCR extracts structured text from scanned/digital PDFs
  • 03 Clean — Gemini AI strips non-narrable content, normalizes text for speech
  • 04 Narrate — Sarvam AI Bulbul V3 TTS generates natural narration
  • 05 Deliver — Audio files assembled with metadata, quality-checked

Tech Stack

  • Sarvam AI Bulbul V3 for Text-to-Speech
  • Mathpix for OCR (scanned + digital PDFs)
  • Google Gemini 2.5 Flash for text cleanup
  • Supabase (PostgreSQL) for data + storage
  • Vercel serverless for auto-scaling deployment

Built For Government

developed in response to an EOI from the Publications Division, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting, Government of India. designed to digitize 200+ government publications as audiobooks.

Capabilities

  • 11 Indian languages — Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Gujarati, Odia, Punjabi, English
  • 25+ natural voice options with pace & temperature control
  • Batch processing for 200+ books at scale
  • ~30-100x cheaper than human narration

HeatChase — Browser Game

a fast-paced browser chase game where you dodge AI-controlled pursuers across an increasingly chaotic map. physics-based movement, dynamic difficulty scaling, and particle effects — all running in real-time in the browser. built with vanilla js and canvas.

JavaScriptCanvas APIGame Dev

How It Works

  • you control a player trying to survive as AI chasers hunt you down
  • physics-based movement with momentum, acceleration, and collision detection
  • difficulty ramps up over time — more chasers, faster speeds, tighter spaces
  • particle effects for explosions, trails, and visual feedback
  • high score tracking and instant restart for that "one more run" addiction

Tech Details

  • custom physics engine for smooth 60fps gameplay

  • responsive design — plays on desktop and mobile
